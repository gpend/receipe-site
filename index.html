Entrees

Crock-pot spaghetti

  • 1 large green pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 clove garlic(clove not head)
  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 1 t salt (optional)
  • red and/or black pepper to taste
  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 c grated carrots
  • 1 No. 2 can Tomatoes
  • 1 No. 2 can Water
  • 1 small can tomato paste
  • 1 T Oregano
  • Dash of catsup
  • Dash of Worcestershire sauce

Chop pepper, onion, and garlic, brown in oil in medium skillet, when onions are translucent, add ground beef. Cook beef and chop into small bits. Bring to Boil - "High" setting on crock pot, then Simmer - 3 hours Add 8 oz spaghetti, Cook on "Low" setting of crock pot Makes lots, can be frozen, do not overcook spaghetti.
